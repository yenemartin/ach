# Compliance Boundary

## V1 Safety Rule

ACH v1 is a marketing and lead-capture platform. It should not collect or expose protected resident-care or medical workflow data.

## Allowed v1 Data

- business listing details
- amenities
- room descriptions
- availability status
- promotions
- public contact details
- contact and tour request information

## Data To Avoid In v1

- resident records
- diagnoses
- medications
- care plans
- charting notes
- staff scheduling data tied to care delivery
- billing, claims, payroll, or compliance records
- uploaded medical or admissions documents

## Form Boundary

Public forms should request only:

- name
- email
- phone
- move-in timeline
- preferred contact method
- preferred tour datetime
- optional message

Do not ask for medical history, medications, or protected care details in v1.
